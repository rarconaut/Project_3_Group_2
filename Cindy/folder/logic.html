    Who Are the Data Students? UCSD & Berkeley

    AT 78.8%, UCSD data students was able to answer more surveys compared to Berkeley students. The biggest factor for this high survey rate is that UCSD has a total of 33 students while Berkeley has only 17. Only 15% of Berkeley students finished the survey while 81% of students from UCSD finshed the survey.

    For our survey, we wanted to observe the gender distribution in both courses. Interestingly, we only had a slightly higher male population in our data class. The score was 18 Males vs. 16 Females.

    The majority of data students are business finance majors while actual data science majors made it in sencond with 6 students and sciences such as bio made it third in the survey.

    Half of the students finished their under grad, while 41.2% were able to finish their graduate programs.
